🏆 Standings tiebreakers

When two or more teams are level on points, the following criteria apply in order:

  1. Points
  2. Goal difference (all league matches)
  3. Goals scored (all league matches)
  4. Head-to-head points (only between tied teams)
  5. Head-to-head goal difference
  6. Head-to-head goals scored
  7. Alphabetical order
